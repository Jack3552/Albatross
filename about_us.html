About Us

Founded in 1998 by Yetchi, Jack and Sakib, Albatross is the leading international service provider in flight and accommodation booking.

Albatross' hotel search allows users to compare hotel prices in just a few clicks for over 4 hotels in 1 city. With 20 vists annually to our site, travellers regularly use the hotel comparison to compare deals in the same city.

Our staff at Albatross are fully committed to finding the lowest price for you and providing excellent customer service.
